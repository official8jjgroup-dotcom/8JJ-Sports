# [Ludvig Åberg Leads The Players Championship as Schauffele Stars in Contention](https://8jjsports.com/ludvig-aberg-leads-the-players-as-schauffele/)

Sweden’s **Ludvig Åberg stormed into the lead at the 2026 Players Championship** at TPC Sawgrass after firing a spectacular **63 in the second round**, leaving him two shots clear of the competition. The 26‑year‑old was electric on the course, including multiple birdies and eagles that set him apart from the field. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

American star **Xander Schauffele also delivered a strong performance**, carding a 7‑under‑par 65 and positioning himself well early in the tournament. Schauffele hit a high percentage of fairways and showed excellent ball‑striking throughout the round. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

TPC Sawgrass, often dubbed the *“fifth major”*, provided a stern test of golf with its iconic island green and challenging layout. The leaderboard battle saw several top players — including Cameron Young, Justin Thomas and Rory McIlroy — remain within striking distance heading into the weekend. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
