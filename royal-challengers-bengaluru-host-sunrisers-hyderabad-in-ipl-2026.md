# [Royal Challengers Bengaluru Host Sunrisers Hyderabad in IPL 2026 Opener](https://8jjsports.com/royal-challengers-bengaluru-host/)

**Defending IPL champions Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB)** are set to host **Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH)** in the **IPL 2026 season opener**, scheduled for **March 28 at the M. Chinnaswamy Stadium** in Bengaluru. The match marks the official start of the highly anticipated 19th edition of the Indian Premier League. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Fans are eagerly awaiting the clash, which will kick off an opening block of fixtures running through early April, with the full schedule to be announced once election dates in several Indian states are confirmed. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Due to last season’s celebrations that followed RCB’s maiden IPL title, local authorities have mandated safety inspections and expert committee approval of the Chinnaswamy Stadium before matches can proceed, with drills and reviews planned ahead of the tournament. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

The opening weekend of IPL 2026 will also feature other key clashes, with franchises gearing up to chase early momentum in what promises to be a competitive season. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
