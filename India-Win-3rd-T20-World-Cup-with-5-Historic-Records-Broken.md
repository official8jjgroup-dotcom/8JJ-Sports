India’s powerful batting lineup and disciplined bowling attack played a crucial role in their championship run. Key performances from top players ensured consistent victories against some of the world’s strongest teams.

This triumph further strengthens India’s legacy in T20 cricket and demonstrates the team’s ability to perform under pressure on the global stage.
