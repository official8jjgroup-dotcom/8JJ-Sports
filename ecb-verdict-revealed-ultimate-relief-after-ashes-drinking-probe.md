# [ECB Verdict Brings Closure to Ashes Drinking Allegations](https://8jj.com/ecb-verdict-revealed/)

The long-awaited ECB verdict on reports of excessive drinking by England players during the Ashes tour has finally brought clarity and relief within the camp. After weeks of scrutiny and speculation, the England and Wales Cricket Board completed its internal investigation and determined that no formal misconduct occurred during the controversial break between the second and third Ashes Tests in Australia.  

The ruling draws a line under a distracting episode for English cricket, allowing attention to return to rebuilding confidence, unity, and on-field performance as the team looks ahead to future challenges.
