# David Warner Charged: Shocking Drink‑Driving Case Explained

Cricket star **David Warner** has been charged with **drink‑driving** following an incident in **Sydney, Australia**, that has raised widespread concern across the cricketing world.

## What Happened

On **April 5, 2026**, Warner was stopped by **New South Wales Police** in the **Maroubra** area during a random roadside breath test. Police reportedly conducted the stop around **5:30 PM** on a Sunday evening. Warner’s **blood alcohol level was recorded at 0.104**, which is **more than twice the legal limit** allowed for drivers in New South Wales. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Officers then arrested Warner and took him to **Maroubra Police Station**, where a secondary breath analysis reportedly confirmed the initial result. He was subsequently **charged with a mid‑range drink‑driving offence** and issued a **court attendance notice**. Warner is scheduled to appear before the **Downing Centre Local Court on May 7, 2026** to answer the charge. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Warner, aged **39**, retired from international cricket in 2024 after a decorated career with **Australia**, but continues to play in T20 franchise leagues.
