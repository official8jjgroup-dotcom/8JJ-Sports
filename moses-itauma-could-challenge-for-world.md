# [Moses Itauma Could Challenge for World Title After Rapid Rise in Heavyweight Division](https://8jj.com/moses-itauma-could-challenge-for-world/)

British heavyweight sensation Moses Itauma is increasingly being viewed as a potential world title contender after an impressive start to his professional boxing career.

The 21-year-old prospect has built a strong reputation in the heavyweight division with a series of dominant performances. Itauma has quickly climbed the rankings and currently holds an undefeated professional record, with many of his victories coming by knockout.

Promoter Frank Warren believes the young fighter could soon challenge for a world heavyweight championship if he continues his current form. According to Warren, the immediate focus is on Itauma’s upcoming bout against American contender Jermaine Franklin. A convincing victory could move him closer to a major title opportunity.

Despite being early in his professional career, Itauma has already attracted attention across the boxing world because of his speed, power and composure in the ring. Many analysts see him as one of the most exciting heavyweight prospects of his generation.

The heavyweight division remains one of the most competitive in boxing, currently led by champions such as Oleksandr Usyk, while other contenders like Fabio Wardley and Daniel Dubois continue to battle for titles and rankings.

If Itauma continues to develop at his current pace, he could soon join the list of fighters challenging for world honours and potentially become one of the youngest heavyweight champions in modern boxing history.
