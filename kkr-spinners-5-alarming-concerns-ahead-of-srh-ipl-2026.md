## Lack of Wicket-Taking Impact

KKR’s spinners have struggled to pick up wickets consistently in recent matches. The lack of breakthroughs in middle overs has allowed opponents to build strong partnerships.

## Overdependence on Key Bowlers

The team continues to rely heavily on a limited number of spin options, making their attack predictable and easier to counter for opposition batters.

## Pressure from Injuries in Pace Unit

Injuries to key fast bowlers have increased the burden on the spin department, forcing them to bowl in less favorable situations. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

## Ineffectiveness in High-Scoring Matches

On flat pitches, the spinners have struggled to control the run rate. Opponents have attacked them aggressively, reducing their overall impact.

## Tactical Challenges in Team Balance

With changing combinations and form issues, KKR have not been able to find the ideal balance between spin and pace, affecting their overall bowling strategy.
