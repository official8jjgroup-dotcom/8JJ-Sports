# [Mitchell Santner Proud of Black Caps Effort Despite T20 World Cup Setback](https://8jjsports.com/mitchell-santner-proud-of-black-caps-effort-despite/)

**New Zealand captain Mitchell Santner expressed pride in his team’s effort**, despite a frustrating defeat that complicated the Black Caps’ path in the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2026.

Santner admitted that the narrow loss to England proved costly, acknowledging that a victory would have made New Zealand’s route to the semi-finals far more straightforward. Instead, the result left the Black Caps relying on other outcomes to determine their fate in the tournament. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

The closely contested match saw New Zealand post a competitive total of around 160. However, Santner suggested the side may have fallen slightly short of a winning score given the conditions, noting that a target closer to 170 or 175 might have significantly changed the result. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

England’s disciplined bowling attack restricted New Zealand’s scoring opportunities during the final overs, preventing the Black Caps from accelerating when it mattered most. The inability to maximize the death overs ultimately proved to be a decisive turning point in the contest. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Santner also highlighted how tight margins often define matches in major tournaments like the T20 World Cup. Small moments — a missed boundary opportunity, a wicket at a key stage, or a well-executed over — can dramatically alter the outcome of a game at this level. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

Despite the disappointment, the New Zealand skipper praised his side’s overall performance throughout the campaign, emphasizing the resilience and effort shown by the players. Santner noted that the team had produced strong phases of cricket but acknowledged they were unable to maintain momentum consistently across the entire match.

With England already securing their place in the semi-finals, New Zealand’s qualification scenario became dependent on the final group results and net run rate calculations involving Pakistan. The Black Caps completed their Super 8 campaign with three points and a healthy net run rate but had to wait to see whether their position would hold. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

Santner’s candid reflection underscored the unforgiving nature of T20 cricket, where a single result can reshape an entire tournament campaign. For New Zealand, the defeat served as a reminder of the fine margins that often separate progression from elimination on the global stage.
