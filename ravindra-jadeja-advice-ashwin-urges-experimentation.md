# [Ravindra Jadeja Advice: Ashwin Urges Experimentation](https://8jj.com/ravindra-jadeja-advice-ashwin-urges-experimentation/)

Ravindra Jadeja received candid advice from former India spinner Ravichandran Ashwin following another tough ODI outing against New Zealand. In the final match at Indore, Jadeja scored 16 off 12 balls and conceded 41 runs in six overs without taking a wicket.

His struggles have continued since the ICC Champions Trophy 2025, managing just one wicket in six ODIs along with modest returns with the bat. Ashwin reportedly urged Jadeja to experiment more with his bowling variations and batting approach to regain his effectiveness at the international level.
