## What’s Happening?

Franchise leagues are now competing harder than ever for top talent, and IPL remains the most attractive destination for players worldwide. With bigger contracts, global exposure, and elite competition, it’s no surprise that players are leaning towards IPL opportunities.

---

## 7 Reasons Behind the Possible Exodus

1. **Higher financial rewards in IPL** – The salary gap continues to widen.  
2. **Global visibility** – IPL offers unmatched international exposure.  
3. **Stronger competition level** – Players want to test themselves against the best.  
4. **Scheduling conflicts** – Overlapping windows force players to choose.  
5. **Franchise stability** – IPL teams offer long-term security and structure.  
6. **Star influence** – Playing alongside legends boosts player careers.  
7. **Career growth opportunities** – IPL performances often open doors to global leagues.

---

## Impact on PSL

If the trend continues, PSL could struggle to retain top international stars, which may affect its competitiveness and global appeal. However, it also opens the door for emerging players to step up and make a name for themselves.

---

## Final Thoughts

The battle between franchise leagues is only getting more intense. IPL 2026 could mark another turning point, not just for Indian cricket, but for the entire T20 ecosystem.
