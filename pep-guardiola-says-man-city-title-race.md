# [Pep Guardiola Says Man City Title Race Could Be Over Without Key Win](https://8jjsports.com/pep-guardiola-says-man-city-title-race/)

Manchester City boss **Pep Guardiola** has issued a stark warning about his team’s Premier League title hopes, saying the title race could effectively be over if City fail to secure victory in their next fixture against West Ham. City sit in **second place in the Premier League**, but trailing leaders Arsenal and with a demanding schedule ahead, Guardiola stressed there is little margin for error. 

Guardiola’s side were recently beaten **3–0 by Real Madrid in the Champions League**, and the City boss stated that his full focus now is on the league, where every match could define their title challenge. 

“Now it’s West Ham that defines the Premier League,” Guardiola said, emphasising that dropping points could make the title race almost unreachable for his team. 

City dropped points in a recent **draw at home**, leaving them with a deficit that requires a near‑perfect run to close the gap on current leaders. 

Despite the warning, Guardiola has often rallied his squad in high‑pressure situations, and supporters will be watching closely to see how Manchester City respond on the pitch in the coming weeks.
