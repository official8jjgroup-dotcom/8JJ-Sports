# [Sanju Samson Named Player of the Tournament as India Win T20 World Cup](https://8jjsports.com/sanju-samson-player-of-the-tournament-as-india-win/)

**Sanju Samson capped off a remarkable campaign by being named Player of the Tournament** as India secured the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup title after a dominant run through the competition.

The Indian wicketkeeper-batter played several decisive innings across the tournament, providing the backbone of India’s batting lineup during crucial matches. His aggressive yet controlled approach helped the team maintain momentum in high-pressure situations.

One of Samson’s standout contributions came during the semi-final against England, where he produced a powerful unbeaten **89 runs from just 42 balls**, anchoring India’s massive total and setting up a thrilling victory. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Throughout the tournament, Samson combined fearless strokeplay with smart shot selection, frequently accelerating the scoring rate during the middle and death overs. His ability to rotate strike and launch boundaries at key moments proved invaluable for India’s attacking strategy.

India’s batting unit consistently produced strong totals, often supported by contributions from players such as Shivam Dube, Ishan Kishan, Hardik Pandya, and Tilak Varma during important matches. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

While Samson’s batting performances grabbed headlines, India’s bowling attack also played a critical role in the team’s success. Fast bowler Jasprit Bumrah delivered crucial spells under pressure, especially during knockout matches when opponents attempted aggressive run chases. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

The combination of explosive batting and disciplined bowling ultimately helped India dominate the tournament and secure the world title. Samson’s consistency and impact across multiple matches made him the standout performer of the competition.

Winning the **Player of the Tournament award** represents a major milestone in Samson’s international career. For a player who has often faced scrutiny regarding consistency, the T20 World Cup campaign served as a powerful reminder of his match-winning potential on the global stage.

India’s triumph further strengthens their reputation as one of the most formidable teams in world T20 cricket, while Samson’s performances have firmly established him as a central figure in the team’s white-ball future.
