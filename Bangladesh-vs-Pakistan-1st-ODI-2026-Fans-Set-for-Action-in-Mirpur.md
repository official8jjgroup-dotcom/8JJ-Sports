# Bangladesh vs Pakistan 1st ODI 2026: Watch the Action in Mirpur

Cricket fans are set for an exciting contest as **Bangladesh and Pakistan face off in the 1st ODI of their 2026 series** at the **Sher-e-Bangla National Cricket Stadium in Mirpur**.

The match marks the start of a **three-match ODI series** between the two Asian teams, with all games scheduled to be played in Mirpur.

Both teams will be looking to build strong momentum in the 50-over format as they prepare for future international competitions, including the upcoming **ICC Cricket World Cup cycle**.

Bangladesh enters the series hoping to take advantage of familiar home conditions, while Pakistan will aim to deliver a strong performance on the road. With several experienced players and emerging talents in both squads, fans can expect **competitive cricket and thrilling moments throughout the series**.

The opening match is scheduled to begin on **March 11**, followed by the remaining ODIs on **March 13 and March 15**, making the Mirpur series a key event in the international cricket calendar.
