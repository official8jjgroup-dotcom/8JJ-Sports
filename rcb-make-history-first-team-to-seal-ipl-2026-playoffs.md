# RCB Make History as First Team to Seal IPL 2026 Playoffs

Royal Challengers Bengaluru have officially become the first team to secure qualification for the IPL 2026 playoffs.

After years of inconsistent campaigns and near misses, RCB have produced one of their most complete league-stage performances in franchise history. Their consistency with both bat and ball has allowed them to dominate crucial matches during the second half of the season.

Virat Kohli’s outstanding form has played a major role in Bengaluru’s success, while the bowling attack has consistently delivered under pressure during tight contests. The team’s improved balance and composure have transformed them into serious title contenders heading into the knockout stage.

The playoff qualification also represents a major moment for the franchise’s supporters, who have waited years to see RCB establish themselves as one of the most dominant teams in the tournament.

With momentum firmly on their side, Bengaluru will now focus on securing a top-two finish as they continue chasing their first IPL championship.
