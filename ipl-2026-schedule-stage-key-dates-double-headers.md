# [IPL 2026 Schedule, Key Dates, and Double-Headers](https://8jj.com/ipl-2026-schedule-stage-key-dates-double-headers/)

The **Indian Premier League (IPL) 2026** is set to run from **March 28 to May 24, 2026**, featuring multiple venues across India and several double-header days that promise non-stop T20 action.

### 📅 Season Timeline
- **Opening match:** Royal Challengers Bengaluru vs Sunrisers Hyderabad on **March 28, 2026** in Bengaluru.  
- **League stage:** Runs through **May 24, 2026**, after which playoffs will determine the champion.

### 🏟️ Venues & Format
- Matches will be played across **12 venues**, including Bengaluru, Mumbai, Chennai, Kolkata, Delhi, Hyderabad, Lucknow, Jaipur, and Raipur.  
- Teams compete in a **round-robin format** before advancing to playoffs.

### 🕒 Double-Header Days
Double-headers are days where **two full matches** are played — an afternoon game and an evening game — providing fans with extended T20 action.

- **Afternoon match:** Starts around **3:30 PM IST**  
- **Evening match:** Starts around **7:30 PM IST**  
- **Eight double-header days** are scheduled in IPL 2026, mostly on weekends.

These double-headers allow teams to complete more fixtures in the available season window while giving broadcasters and fans extra entertainment.
