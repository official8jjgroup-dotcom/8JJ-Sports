# Hardik Pandya Stars As India Crush South Africa By 101 Runs, SA Record Lowest T20I Total

Hardik Pandya made a spectacular comeback to international cricket, smashing an unbeaten 59 off 28 balls and taking a key wicket as India thrashed South Africa by 101 runs in the first T20I at Cuttack. South Africa were bowled out for 74, their lowest-ever T20I total, surpassing their previous record of 87 all out against India in 2022
