# IPL 2026 Caps Race: Jaiswal & Bishnoi Dominate Top 5

As the **Indian Premier League (IPL) 2026** season heats up, the battle for individual honours is taking centre stage with **Orange Cap** and **Purple Cap** chases providing plenty of excitement.

## Jaiswal Leading the Orange Cap Charge

**Yashasvi Jaiswal** has surged to the front of the **Orange Cap** leaderboard with a string of high‑impact innings. His ability to score big runs consistently — often at a rapid pace — has put him ahead of other top batters, making him the favourite for the season’s top run‑scorer award.

## Bishnoi Among Purple Cap Leaders

In the race for the **Purple Cap**, **Roosh Bishnoi** has cemented his place in the top‑5 wicket‑takers through disciplined bowling and smart use of conditions. His knack for taking timely wickets has made him one of the most effective bowlers in the tournament so far.

## Other Top Contenders

### 1. Orange Cap Chasers
A handful of explosive batters sit just behind Jaiswal in the Orange Cap race, ready to challenge if they produce match‑winning scores in upcoming matches.

### 2. Purple Cap Chasers  
Similarly, several bowlers are in close contention with Bishnoi for the Purple Cap, making the fight for most wickets a hotly contested battle.

## Importance of Momentum

At this point in the season, maintaining consistent form is key. Players like Jaiswal and Bishnoi have built early momentum, giving them an edge as the season progresses.

## What Fans Can Expect

With more matches on the schedule, both the Orange and Purple Cap races are sure to stay competitive. Fans will be watching closely to see if Jaiswal can maintain his Orange Cap lead and whether Bishnoi or other bowlers can overtake the honours for most wickets.
