# T20 World Cup: 5 Big Reasons Gambhir Rejects Personal Milestones

India’s cricket strategy for the T20 World Cup continues to emphasize one clear philosophy — **team success over individual milestones**. Head coach Gautam Gambhir has repeatedly highlighted that personal records and statistics mean little if they don’t contribute to winning matches and trophies.

This mindset reflects a larger cultural shift in Indian cricket, where collective performance and match impact are valued more than personal achievements.
