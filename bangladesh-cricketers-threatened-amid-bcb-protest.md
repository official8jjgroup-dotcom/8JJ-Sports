# [Bangladesh Cricketers Threatened Amid BCB Protest](https://8jj.com/bangladesh-cricketers-threatened-amid-bcb-protest/)

---

Bangladesh cricketers have faced an alarming backlash after **protesting against the Bangladesh Cricket Board (BCB)** following controversial remarks by director **M. Nazmul Islam**.  

The dispute began when Nazmul labelled former captain **Tamim Iqbal** an **"Indian agent"**, prompting widespread outrage among players and fans alike. The controversy escalated into a **nationwide player boycott**, putting the BCB under intense scrutiny.  

The incident has raised serious concerns about **player safety** and governance in Bangladesh cricket, with calls for urgent dialogue to resolve the conflict and restore stability in the sport.
