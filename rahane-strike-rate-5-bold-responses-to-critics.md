# [Rahane Strike Rate: 5 Bold Responses to Critics](https://8jj.com/rahane-strike-rate-5-bold-responses-to-critics/)

Veteran Indian batter **Ajinkya Rahane** has faced criticism over his strike rate in the modern game, especially in T20 formats where quick scoring is often glorified. In response, Rahane and his advocates have offered several strong rebuttals that put his approach in context.

Here are **five bold responses** to critics of his strike rate:

---

### 🔥 1. Context Matters Most  
Rahane has emphasised that scoring rate should always be judged in light of the match situation and role assigned by the team, rather than as a standalone metric.

---

### 🧠 2. Stability Over Haste  
Instead of prioritising instant aggression, Rahane’s approach focuses on building partnerships and anchoring the innings — a strategy that has helped his teams in key moments across formats.

---

### 📊 3. Value Beyond Numbers  
Supporters have pointed out that strike rate doesn’t capture crucial contributions such as rotating strike, absorbing pressure, and creating platforms for other batters to thrive.

---

### 💬 4. Backed by Team Leadership  
Coaches and senior players have publicly backed Rahane’s role, underlining that his experience and cricketing intelligence add depth to the batting order beyond raw scoring figures.

---

### 💪 5. Evolution, Not Rigidity  
Rahane has said that while he respects modern scoring demands, he also evolves his game where needed — blending experience with adaptability rather than chasing strike rate alone.

---

The debate around Rahane reflects a larger conversation in modern cricket about balancing traditional batting techniques with the demands of fast-paced formats.
