# Mohamed Salah Confirms Liverpool Exit at End of 2025–26 Season as ‘Egyptian King’ Era Nears Its End

Liverpool superstar **Mohamed Salah** has confirmed that he will leave the club at the end of the **2025–26 season**, ending one of the most iconic individual careers in the modern history of **English football**.

The Egyptian forward, widely known as the **“Egyptian King,”** made the announcement through an emotional message that marks the beginning of the end of his celebrated spell at **Anfield**.

## End of an Era at Anfield

Salah’s departure brings to a close a remarkable **nine-year spell at Liverpool**, during which he became one of the club’s greatest-ever players.

Since arriving from Roma in 2017, he has been central to Liverpool’s modern success, helping the club secure major honours including the **Premier League, UEFA Champions League, FA Cup, and Club World Cup**.

He leaves as one of the highest scorers in the club’s history, with his legacy firmly embedded in Liverpool’s modern identity.

## A Defining Figure of the Klopp and Slot Eras

Salah’s influence has spanned multiple managerial eras, from **Jürgen Klopp’s title-winning project** to the transition into **Arne Slot’s leadership**.

Across those years, he has consistently delivered elite-level performances, combining goals, assists, and decisive moments in key matches across domestic and European competitions.

Even in seasons where team performance fluctuated, Salah remained Liverpool’s most reliable attacking force.

## Record-Breaking Legacy

Over his Liverpool career, Salah has achieved numerous milestones, including:

- Multiple **Premier League Golden Boots**
- Over **200 goals for the club**
- Key contributor in **Champions League triumph**
- Numerous club records for scoring consistency and attacking output

His numbers place him among the greatest forwards in Premier League history and one of the most influential African players ever to play in Europe.

## Liverpool Prepare for Transition

Salah’s exit raises major questions for Liverpool’s long-term attacking structure. Replacing his output, leadership, and consistency will be one of the club’s most significant challenges in the post-Salah era.

While Liverpool are expected to plan ahead for his departure, his influence on and off the pitch will be difficult to replicate.

## What Comes Next for Salah

With his Liverpool chapter closing, speculation is already growing around his next destination, with **Saudi Pro League and MLS clubs** among those reportedly monitoring his situation.

However, no official decision has been made regarding his next move beyond Anfield.

## A Legacy That Redefined an Era

Salah’s departure marks the end of one of the most productive and iconic spells in Liverpool’s modern history.

His goals, consistency, and defining moments have shaped a generation of success at the club — and his legacy will remain central to Liverpool’s identity long after he leaves.
