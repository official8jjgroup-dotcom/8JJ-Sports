# [Sophia Dunkley Finds Freedom as England Opener in 2026 Season](https://8jjsports.com/sophia-dunkley-finds-freedom-as-england-opener/)

England women’s batter **Sophia Dunkley** has revitalised her game and fully embraced her role as an **opening batter for England**, carrying momentum and confidence into the 2026 cricket season after a standout domestic stint and strong recent performances. Following a productive run in the **Women’s Big Bash League (WBBL)** with the Sydney Sixers — where she scored multiple 40+ scores and three half‑centuries — Dunkley has carried that form into her international commitments and now stands as a consistent top‑order presence for her national side.

Head coach **Charlotte Edwards** has trusted her atop the order alongside partner Danni Wyatt‑Hodge, giving Dunkley a defined role and the freedom to play her natural attacking game, a sharp contrast to previous seasons where she featured more sporadically and in rotation. With a strike rate above 130 and recent scores including an impactful 81* against the West Indies women’s team in a T20 fixture, Dunkley’s opening position is a key strategic piece for England as they prepare for major events such as the home **ICC Women’s T20 World Cup later in 2026**.

Her progression into a reliable top‑order batter has not only strengthened England’s batting depth but also provided stability at the start of innings — an important foundation for success in the shortest format of the game.
