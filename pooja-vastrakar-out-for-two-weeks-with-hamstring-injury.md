# [Pooja Vastrakar Out for Two Weeks With Hamstring Injury](https://8jj.com/pooja-vastrakar-out-for-two-weeks-with-hamstring-injury/)

Pooja Vastrakar, the Royal Challengers Bengaluru all-rounder, has been ruled out of action for two weeks due to a hamstring injury. RCB head coach Malolan Rangarajan confirmed the setback following the opening game of WPL 2026.

The injury comes at a challenging time, adding to fitness concerns within the squad as the team navigates a packed tournament schedule. RCB will now look to adjust their lineup and strategies while hoping for a swift recovery for Vastrakar.
