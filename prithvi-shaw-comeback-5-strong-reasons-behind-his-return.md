# [Prithvi Shaw Comeback: 5 Strong Reasons Behind His Return](https://8jj.com/prithvi-shaw-comeback-5-strong-reasons-behind-his-return/)

Indian batter **Prithvi Shaw** has been making headlines with a strong resurgence in form, and fans are keen to understand the key factors driving his return to prominence. Here are **five strong reasons** behind Shaw’s comeback:

---

### 🔄 1. Refocused Mental Approach

Shaw has spoken about adopting a fresh mindset that prioritises patience, shot selection, and playing the situation — a shift that has steadied his game under pressure.

---

### 🏏 2. Technical Adjustments

Working with batting coaches, Shaw has refined his technique, particularly against short‑ball bowling and against quality pace attacks, improving his consistency at the crease.

---

### 💪 3. Fitness and Endurance Enhancements

Stronger fitness routines have helped Shaw maintain intensity throughout innings and across back‑to‑back matches, reducing early fatigue and improving focus.

---

### 📊 4. Success in Domestic Competitions

Standout performances in domestic cricket — particularly in Ranji Trophy and other first‑class fixtures — have boosted confidence and demonstrated his readiness for higher challenges.

---

### 🤝 5. Supportive Team Environment

Shaw’s recent environments — both in the national setup and in franchise leagues — have emphasised positive support, clear role definitions, and shared goals that have helped him thrive.

---

Prithvi Shaw’s revival is a blend of mental strength, technical improvement, and supportive surroundings — making his comeback one of the most talked‑about stories in cricket this year.
