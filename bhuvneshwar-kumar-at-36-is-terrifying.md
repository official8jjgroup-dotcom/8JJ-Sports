# Bhuvneshwar Kumar at 36 Is Still Terrifying Batters

At 36 years old, Bhuvneshwar Kumar is producing one of the finest bowling seasons of his IPL career.

The experienced Indian pacer has become one of the most feared bowlers in IPL 2026 thanks to his remarkable control, swing, and ability to perform under pressure. While many expected younger fast bowlers to dominate the season, Bhuvneshwar has once again shown why experience remains invaluable in T20 cricket.

His performances with the new ball and at the death have consistently shifted momentum in favor of his team. Batters have struggled against his subtle variations, disciplined lengths, and calm execution during high-pressure overs.

Bhuvneshwar’s success has also highlighted the importance of intelligent pace bowling in modern T20 cricket, where raw speed alone is often not enough. His ability to outthink batters continues to separate him from many bowlers in the tournament.

As IPL 2026 moves closer to the playoffs, Bhuvneshwar Kumar’s incredible form could become one of the defining factors in the race for the title.
