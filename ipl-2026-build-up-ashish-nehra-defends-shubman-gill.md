# Ashish Nehra Defends [Shubman Gill](https://8jjsports.com/player-profile/?player_id=3362) Amid T20I Criticism Ahead Of IPL 2026

Shubman Gill has found a staunch supporter in Gujarat Titans head coach **Ashish Nehra**, who has cautioned against snap judgments based on a couple of T20I outings.

Nehra stressed that judging players after just two matches in a fast-paced format like **T20 cricket** can be misleading and reflects a growing trend of forming quick conclusions in Indian cricket.

## Key Points

- Ashish Nehra backed [Shubman Gill](https://8jjsports.com/player-profile/?player_id=3362) amid criticism of his recent T20I performances  
- He highlighted the dangers of evaluating players on very small sample sizes  
- Emphasized that T20 cricket demands patience and context before passing judgment  
- Comments come ahead of **IPL 2026**, where Gill remains a key figure
