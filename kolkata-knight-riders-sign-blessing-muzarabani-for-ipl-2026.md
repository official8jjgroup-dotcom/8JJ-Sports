# [Kolkata Knight Riders Sign Blessing Muzarabani for IPL 2026](https://8jjsports.com/kolkata-knight-riders-sign-blessing-muzarabani/)

**Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR)** have strengthened their bowling lineup for the **Indian Premier League (IPL) 2026** season by signing tall Zimbabwean fast bowler **Blessing Muzarabani** as a replacement in their squad. The 6 ft 8 in quick brings significant international experience and a reputation for extracting steep bounce from pitches, enhancing KKR’s pace attack ahead of the tournament. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Muzarabani joins KKR after concluding a strong campaign in the **ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2026**, where he was one of Zimbabwe’s standout performers with several key wickets. His arrival comes after the franchise released Bangladesh pacer Mustafizur Rahman from their IPL roster, following team adjustments earlier this year. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

The signing is expected to bolster KKR’s bowling resources as they prepare for their opening matches of the season, combining Muzarabani’s height and pace with the team’s established bowling talent. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
