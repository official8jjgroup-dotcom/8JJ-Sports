# Hardik Pandya and MI: How IPL 2026 Fell Apart

Mumbai Indians’ IPL 2026 campaign has turned into one of the franchise’s most disappointing seasons in recent years.

Despite entering the tournament with a star-studded squad and high expectations, MI struggled to build consistency throughout the campaign. Injuries, poor starts, and repeated batting collapses placed the team under pressure from the opening weeks of the season.

Much of the spotlight has fallen on captain Hardik Pandya, whose leadership decisions and on-field performances have faced heavy criticism from fans and cricket experts alike. Questions surrounding team balance, player roles, and dressing-room chemistry only intensified as losses continued to pile up.

The five-time champions appeared unable to recover momentum, with several close defeats further damaging their playoff hopes. As frustration grew among supporters, discussions about the future direction of the franchise became a major talking point across the cricket world.

IPL 2026 may ultimately be remembered as a season where Mumbai Indians failed to find rhythm, confidence, and stability when it mattered most.
