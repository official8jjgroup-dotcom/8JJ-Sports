# [New Zealand Women Cricket Team Crush South Africa in T20I Opener](https://8jjsports.com/new-zealand-women-cricket-team-crush-south-africa/)

The **New Zealand women’s cricket team** produced a commanding display to open their **five‑match T20I series against South Africa**, securing an **80‑run victory** at **Bay Oval in Tauranga on March 15, 2026**. New Zealand posted a strong total of **190 for 7**, powered by **Amelia Kerr’s 78 off 44 balls** and **Georgia Plimmer’s 63**, helping set a challenging target. ([turn0news15][turn0reddit47])

In reply, South Africa were restricted to **110 for 7**, unable to build momentum against disciplined bowling from the hosts. **Sophie Devine led the attack with 4 wickets for just 12 runs**, and **Jess Kerr chipped in with two key dismissals**, guiding New Zealand to an emphatic win in the series opener. ([turn0news1][turn0reddit49])

The victory underlines the **White Ferns’ balance of powerful batting and incisive bowling early in the tour**, setting positive momentum as the series continues across major New Zealand venues. ([turn0news0][turn0news5])
