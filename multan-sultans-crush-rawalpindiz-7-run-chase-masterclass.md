# Multan Sultans Crush Rawalpindiz in 7‑Run Chase Masterclass

In a dominant **Pakistan Super League (PSL) 2026** performance, **Multan Sultans** chased down a competitive target of **183 runs** with ease, defeating **Rawalpindiz** by **seven wickets** at the **Gaddafi Stadium in Lahore**. The chase was wrapped up emphatically **in just 16.2 overs**, highlighting the strength and balance of the Sultans’ batting lineup. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

After being put into bat, Rawalpindiz posted a respectable **182/8**, anchored by a solid **56 not out from Sam Billings** and a quick 35 from Kamran Ghulam. However, regular wickets prevented them from posting a truly challenging total. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Multan’s reply was clinical. **Sahibzada Farhan led the charge with a powerful 68 off 38 balls**, while **Josh Philippe contributed 56 off 30**, the pair forging a match‑winning **110‑run partnership** that put the chase firmly in their favour. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

With that foundation laid, **Shan Masood (27 not out)** and **captain Ashton Turner (26 not out)** saw Multan home with comfortable margins to spare. Their measured finishing ensured the target was reached with **22 balls remaining**. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

Earlier in the match, Multan’s bowling unit, particularly **Faisal Akram**, was instrumental in limiting Rawalpindiz’s scoring. Akram finished with excellent figures and was named **Player of the Match** for his influential performance with the ball. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

This victory underlined Multan Sultans’ credentials as one of the tournament favourites in PSL 2026, showcasing strong batting depth and disciplined bowling. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
