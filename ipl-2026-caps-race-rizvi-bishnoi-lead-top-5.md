# IPL 2026 Caps Race: Rizvi & Bishnoi Lead Top 5

As **IPL 2026** reaches its midpoint, the race for individual honours has taken centre stage — particularly in the **Orange (most runs)** and **Purple (most wickets)** Cap contests.

## Orange Cap: Sameer Rizvi Dominates

Right now, **Sameer Rizvi** sits comfortably at the top of the Orange Cap leaderboard thanks to a string of impactful innings. His consistency and ability to score quickly have put him ahead of fellow contenders, allowing him to build a strong lead over the competition.

## Purple Cap: Bishnoi Among Leaders

In the battle for the Purple Cap, **Roosh Kalaria (Bishnoi)** — known for his accuracy and ability to trouble batters in all phases — has made his way into the top‑5 wicket‑takers. With several matches still to play, his consistent performances have made him one of the leading bowlers to watch.

## Other Top Performers

### 1. Other Orange Cap Contenders  
- A couple of explosive batters remain close behind Rizvi, threatening to challenge his lead if they produce big scores in upcoming games.

### 2. Purple Cap Chasers  
- A handful of fast bowlers and spinners are clustered just behind Bishnoi, and a couple of strong performances could see the Purple Cap change hands.

## Importance of Mid‑Season Form

Maintaining consistency is crucial at this stage of the tournament. With more games ahead, staying at the top of the caps race requires adaptability, execution under pressure, and big contributions across matches.

## What Comes Next

As the IPL season progresses, fans will be watching closely to see if Rizvi can maintain his Orange Cap lead and whether Bishnoi — and other bowlers — can continue their march toward the Purple Cap.
