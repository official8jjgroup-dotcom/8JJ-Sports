# RR Crush CSK by 8 Wickets in Dominant IPL 2026 Clash

**Rajasthan Royals (RR)** delivered a commanding performance to crush **Chennai Super Kings (CSK)** by **8 wickets** in a one-sided IPL 2026 encounter.

The match was dominated by RR from the start, as their bowlers restricted CSK to a **modest total**, setting up a comfortable chase.  

The highlight of the game was a sensational knock from young star **Vaibhav Sooryavanshi**, who smashed a rapid half-century to seal the victory in style. 

His explosive innings, including a **15-ball fifty**, stunned the opposition and shifted momentum completely in RR’s favor.   

RR’s all-round performance, including disciplined bowling and aggressive batting, ensured they controlled the match from start to finish.

This victory gives Rajasthan strong momentum early in the tournament, while CSK will need to regroup after a disappointing performance.
