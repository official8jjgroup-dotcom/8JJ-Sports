# [South Africa Thrash West Indies By 9 Wickets in 1st T20I](https://8jj.com/south-africa-thrash-west-indies-by-9-wickets-in-1st-t20i/)

South Africa delivered a commanding performance to thrash West Indies by nine wickets in the 1st T20I at Boland Park, Paarl, taking a 1-0 lead in the three-match series. The Proteas were clinical in all departments, combining disciplined bowling with explosive batting.

Playing in familiar home conditions, South Africa restricted the West Indies to a modest total before chasing it down comfortably with plenty of overs to spare. Their bowlers set the tone early, applying constant pressure and never allowing the visitors to build momentum.

With the bat, South Africa showcased depth and confidence, underlining their intent early in the series and sending a strong message ahead of the remaining matches.
